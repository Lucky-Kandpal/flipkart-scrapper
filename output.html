    Product Description
    Compact and Lightweight

    Sporting a compact size, the Canon PIXMA MG2570S Inkjet Printer is easy to store and can effortlessly fit on even small shelves and spaces. Furthermore, with its lightweight design, this printer can be easily moved from one place to another.

    Owing to its Auto Power ON feature, this printer is capable of detecting a print command and automatically turning on when a USB cable is connected.
